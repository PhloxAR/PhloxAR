PhloxAR

About

PhloxAR is a rapid prototyping framework for Augmented Reality in Python. Make it easier an pythonic to build Augmented Reality and Computer Vision applications. With it, you can easily build a wonderful AR applications on Linux, Windows, OS X, Android, iOS and Raspberry pi. Also you get access to computer vision libraries from ground up.

Note

PhloxAR is still in the early stages of development.
